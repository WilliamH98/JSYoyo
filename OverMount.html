1. Bring your free hand finger against the string and pull back, so that the yo-yo comes...

2. ...Up...

3. ...and jumps into the string from the front.

4. Give a little slack with your throw hand...

5. ...letting the yo-yo hang in a loop.

6. Cushion the swing a little.

7. Congratulations, you are now in the Over Mount.

8. To dismount, swing the yo-yo towards you and pull up on the string with your throw hand, catapulting the yo-yo...

9. ...out to the front. An alternative to this dismount is to just pull on the string in step 7, performing a Frontstyle Bind.
